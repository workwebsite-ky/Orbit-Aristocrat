WHAT WE DO

Our Capabilities

Full-spectrum aerospace and aeronautics services, engineered to the highest standards of precision and performance.

01

Aeronautics Engineering

Our aeronautics division delivers comprehensive aircraft design, analysis, and integration. From fixed-wing platforms to experimental VTOL configurations, we engineer airframes that push the limits of atmospheric performance.

  • Aerodynamic analysis & CFD simulation
  • Structural design & stress testing
  • Flight control systems integration
  • Certification support (FAA, EASA)
02

Orbital Systems Design

We architect and deploy orbital systems from CubeSats to large geostationary platforms. Our orbital mechanics team ensures mission-optimal trajectories, fuel efficiency, and communication link budgets.

  • Satellite system architecture
  • Orbital transfer & station-keeping
  • Ground station network design
  • Space debris avoidance planning
03

Propulsion Systems

From hypergolic chemical propulsion to next-generation ion drives, our propulsion engineers design systems optimized for specific mission Δv requirements, lifetime, and mass constraints.

  • Liquid & solid propellant systems
  • Electric propulsion (Hall effect, ion)
  • Thruster integration & testing
  • Propellant management systems
04

Avionics & Navigation

Cutting-edge avionics suites, flight computers, and inertial navigation systems developed for both atmospheric and space vehicles, with radiation-hardened options for deep-space missions.

  • Flight management systems
  • Radiation-hardened electronics
  • GNSS & inertial navigation fusion
  • Telemetry & data link systems
05

Space Strategy Consultation

Strategic advisory services for governments, agencies, and private enterprises entering or expanding in the space economy. We shape missions from initial concept through policy and regulatory navigation.

  • Space mission feasibility studies
  • Launch vehicle selection advisory
  • International space law compliance
  • Commercial space market analysis
06

Deep Space Operations

Planning and operations support for missions beyond Earth orbit — lunar, Lagrange point, and interplanetary. We handle the extraordinary complexity of missions where every second of communication latency counts.

  • Interplanetary trajectory design
  • Deep space communication systems
  • Autonomous mission control AI
  • Reentry & landing system design
